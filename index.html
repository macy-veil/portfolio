About

Hi! I'm Macy Veil, a Brazil-based screenwriter.

My work focuses on philosophical science fiction, dystopian narratives, psychological drama, and surreal horror. I prioritize visual storytelling and immersive experiences, using sound design, natural environments, distant human presence, and worldbuilding to construct deeply atmospheric narratives.

Inspirations: My work draws on Kubrick's ambition and Denis Villeneuve's emotional restraint. I'm drawn to subverting expectations, allowing meaning to emerge gradually rather than through explicit exposition. A curated list of Films That Shaped My Voice can be found on Letterboxd.

Vision: I aim to explore bold narrative structures and develop a distinct cinematic language that treats atmosphere, scale, and perception as narrative forces. I approach writing by fully imagining and feeling each scene before committing it to the page — prioritizing clarity, immersion, and intuitive visual flow.

Open to international productions and collaboration with directors who prioritize atmosphere and visual restraint.
